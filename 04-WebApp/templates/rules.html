What is this platform?


This platform provides a tool for job candidates looking for practice regarding interview questions. Our platform allows the candidate to answer typical interview questions either by video or by text. We have developed complex Deep Learning algorithms that analyze the results of the video or the text and provide the candidate a summary of what our algorithm perceived, and how the candidate performed compared to previous candidates.




Why this project?


This project was developed as a final year project for CMR Institute of Technology. The team worked for several months on the individual models and built this platform in the final month. We used Python for model training, Flask for the web interface, and D3.js for visualization purposes.



Who contributed?


The platform was developed by a team of contributors as part of the final year project.
